Chongster Email Signatures

  1. Go to Gmail → Settings → See all settings → General → Signature
  2. Delete your existing signature, including any disclaimer — the disclaimer will now be appended automatically
  3. Click your name below to open your signature file, then press Cmd+A to select all, then Cmd+C to copy
  4. Paste into the Gmail signature editor with Cmd+V
  5. Phone number: if you'd like to include your number, replace the placeholder number with your real number — remember to also update the link behind it (right-click the number → Edit link) to match, e.g. tel:+84123456789 — otherwise, delete that entire row from the signature editor
